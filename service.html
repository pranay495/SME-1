HPL Cladding

HPL cladding The term ' cladding ' refers to components that are attached to the primary structure of a building to form non- structural, external surfaces. This is as opposed to buildings in which the external surfaces are formed by structural elements, such as masonry walls, or applied surfaces such as render.
