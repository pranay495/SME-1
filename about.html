Shree Mahalaxmi Exteriors Group

Shree Mahalaxmi Exterior formed in 2001 with - member team to serve the need of construction industry around Kolhapur, a prominent town in Western Maharashtra. We are specialized in ACP Cladding, Structural Glazing, Spider fittings, Frame-less Glass Doors, Glass Partitions and Aluminum windows etc. Our experienced and well-organized teams of designers, engineers and technicians always leaves its remark with our client. We are proud to say that over the period we have become prominent name in the business of Exterior work in Kolhapur, Southern Maharashtra, North Karnataka and Goa with strong and growing team.
